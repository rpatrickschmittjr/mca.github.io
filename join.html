Membership

We are glad that you are interested in joining the Mayo Civic Association.

While you do not need to be a member to attend meetings and have a voice in the Mayo community, there are some pretty great reasons to make it official:
1. Your membership allows you to vote.
2. Your membership dues helps keep the lights and helps operate and maintain the MCA Community Hall at 1175 Mayo Road.
3. Your membership entitles you or your organization to use the MCA Community Hall for approved public and private meetings and events.
3. Your membership makes you eligible to join the the Association Board.
4. Your membership gets you access to regular communications and updates to what is happening in Mayo.


Community Association Mission:

The mission of the Mayo Civic Association is to lead concerted action to improve the safety, quality of life, general welfare and development of the community for all Greater Mayo Peninsula residents.
